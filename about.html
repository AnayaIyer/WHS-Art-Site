Who are we?


My name is Anaya Iyer.

I've been drawing for as long as I can remember. To this day, I hear stories of me waking up at 6 am as a child to draw (however horribly) my favorite characters. Later, I discovered that I could make my drawings move and decided right then and there that I would become an artist when I grew up.

I've been working nonstop since then to improve my art and design skills. Over this time period, my animation placed top 6 in a challenge, I've built my Instagram page from the ground up, and have had my art displayed in the school lobby.
