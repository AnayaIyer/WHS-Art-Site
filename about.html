Who are we?


Welcome to Westborough High School!

WHS is a public high school in Westborough, Massachusetts. Here, we celebrate student and staff achievements and encourage inclusion in our community.

The WHS Fine Arts program is rich with hands-on experience and creativity, which is why we support up-and-coming student artists as they find their paths.
